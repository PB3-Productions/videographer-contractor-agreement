Videographer / Independent Contractor Agreement

Contract ID:

1. Scope of Work

The Videographer agrees to provide professional videography services as assigned by the Company. Services may include, but are not limited to:

  • Filming events, productions, or other assigned projects
  • Capturing video and audio content according to Company standards
  • Delivering raw footage and/or edited content as directed

The Videographer agrees to perform all services in a professional and timely manner.

2. Independent Contractor Status

The Videographer is engaged as an independent contractor and not as an employee of the Company. The Videographer is responsible for all taxes, insurance, and business expenses unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3. Ownership of Content

All footage, recordings, edits, and any related materials created during the course of work for the Company are considered work made for hire and are the sole property of PB3 Productions.

The Videographer agrees:

  • They have no ownership rights to any content created under this Agreement
  • All materials must be delivered to the Company upon request or completion of the project

4. Non-Use and Non-Distribution of Content

The Videographer may not use, share, publish, or distribute any content created for PB3 Productions without prior written consent.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Posting on personal or business social media accounts
  • Uploading to portfolios, websites, or demo reels
  • Sharing with third parties

Any unauthorized use of content will be considered a material breach of this Agreement.

5. Non-Solicitation

While performing work for PB3 Productions, the Videographer agrees that they will not:

  • Solicit or attempt to solicit business from clients, guests, or contacts of the Company
  • Promote their own services or other businesses while on the job
  • Divert or attempt to divert Company clients for personal gain

This restriction applies during all active job assignments and interactions related to Company projects.

6. Confidentiality

The Videographer agrees to keep all Company information confidential, including:

  • Client identities and details
  • Project information
  • Business practices

This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

7. Compensation

Compensation will be agreed upon per project or assignment and may be outlined in a separate written agreement, invoice, or communication.

8. Equipment and Liability

Unless otherwise specified:

  • The Videographer is responsible for providing their own equipment
  • The Videographer assumes responsibility for any damage, loss, or malfunction of their equipment
  • The Videographer agrees to act safely and professionally at all times.

9. Termination

This Agreement may be terminated by either party at any time with written notice. The Company reserves the right to terminate immediately in the event of:

  • Breach of contract
  • Unprofessional conduct
  • Violation of Company policies

10. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the State of Nevada.

11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.

12. Signatures

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms outlined in this Agreement.
